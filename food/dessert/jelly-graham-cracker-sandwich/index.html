Gortimer's Jelly Graham Cracker Sandwich... That's Quick!

Gortimer made themselves a snack and wanted me to 'put it online for everybody to read.'

Ingredients

  • two 4 cracker sheets Graham crackers (I'd say any but - we exclusively buy Nabisco Honey Maid Grahams)
  • 2T Jelly, Lemon Curd, any sort of sweet spreadable filling

Steps

  1. Break the sheets in half, so they're squares comprised of two crackers.
  2. On two of the squares, spread jelly thinly.
  3. Sandwich them with the other squares.
  4. Wait 10-15 minutes for some of the moisture from the jelly to get into the cracker. You don't want the cracker to be crunchy, it should have a slight resistance and be more like shortbread.
